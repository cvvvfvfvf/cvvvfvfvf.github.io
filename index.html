About me

Hi, I'm Jingyi Li, an undergraduate student pursuing a B.S. in Data Science and Big Data Technology program at the School of Mathematics, Tianjin University (expected graduation: July 2027). Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at both Tianjin University (advised by Dr. Haixiang Zhang) and UNC-Chapel Hill (in the BIG-S2 lab, advised by Dr. Tengfei Li).

High-Dimensional Mediation Analysis in DNA Information Storage

Institution: Tianjin University, Tianjin, China
Role: Research Intern
Advisor: Dr. Haixiang Zhang
Period: Sep. 2025 – Present

  • Achieved robust estimation: in simulations with 10,000 potential mediators, our method attained a 74.5% detection rate for key mediators and reduced estimation bias by up to 12.6% compared to conventional linear approaches.
